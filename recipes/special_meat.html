Ingredients

  • Mustard Oil
  • Meat Masala
  • Salt
  • Ginger
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Spicy Masala
  • Red Chilly

Steps

  1. First burn oil to its extreme
  2. Second Add Red Chilly
  3. Third add Onion
  4. Cook for 5 minutes until Onion color change
  5. Add all Masala and cook for 5 minutes
  6. Add Goad Meat and fry in low heat for 1 hour
  7. Nepali Goat Meat Dish is ready
